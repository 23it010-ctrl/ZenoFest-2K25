About Project Expo

The National Level Project Expo – Zenofest, organized by the Tech Titans Association, Department of Information Technology, PSR Engineering College, Sivakasi an Autonomous institution affiliated to Anna University is one among the best educational institutions in Tamilnadu . It provides a vibrant platform for students to showcase their innovative ideas, technical projects, and creative solutions.

This expo encourages innovation, teamwork, and problem-solving while offering students the opportunity to present their work to faculty, peers, and industry experts. It bridges the gap between theory and practical application, preparing young minds for research, entrepreneurship, and real-world challenges.
